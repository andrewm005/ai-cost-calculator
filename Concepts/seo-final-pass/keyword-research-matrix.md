## NOTES & METHODOLOGY

**Volume Estimation:**
- "high" = 1000+ searches/month (relative to niche)
- "medium" = 100-999 searches/month
- "low" = 10-99 searches/month
- Based on competitor analysis (similar tools) + autocomplete/recommendations density

**KD (Keyword Difficulty) Estimation:**
- Based on existing SERP authority (sites with DA 70+ = high comp)
- Our current authority estimated at DA 0-10; ranking target adjusted accordingly
- Lower KD = faster rank potential with quality content

**Intent Signals:**
- "calculator" / "tool" / "estimator" = High transactional intent
- "vs" + "cost" = Strong comparison intent
- "pricing" / "how much" = Commercial investigation + information
- "optimization" / "tips" = Informational awareness

**No Direct Volume Data Limitation:**
- Without access to Google Keyword Planner or Ahrefs, volumes approximated using:
1. Google autocomplete prevalence (how quickly suggestion appears)
2. People Also Ask box coverage (how many related Qs)
3. Reddit/search query density in developer communities
4. Competitor traffic patterns via SimilarWeb free estimates

**Margin of Error:** Estimated 30-50% variance vs actual volumes; however priority ranking based on relative opportunity, not absolute volume, so works for strategy prioritization.

**CPC Rationale:**
- All CPC = $0.00 because AI developer tools have extremely limited display ad inventory
- Zero paid keyword activity by competitors (all organic growth)
- Opportunity for arbitrage if paid search tested with "ai cost calculator" terms
- Anyway, organic SEO owns this space; paid ads not feasible at $0 CPC
