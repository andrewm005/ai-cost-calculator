About the data

Where do the prices in these articles come from?

Every number in every article comes from the OpenRouter public models endpoint at https://openrouter.ai/api/v1/models, refreshed every six hours on AI Cost Calculator. The 334-model cache is the same data the calculator uses.

Are these prices vendor-direct or include a markup?

The OpenRouter feed adds a small platform fee on top of the upstream vendor's list price, usually a few percent. For most models the difference is well under 10% of the bill. Vendor-direct pricing pages are linked from each model's page on this site.

How often are the articles updated?

Articles are updated when the published pricing changes meaningfully. The "Last updated" date at the top of each article is the date of the most recent review. The model pages are the source of truth for the current rate of any specific model.
