About Morph V3 Large

Morph V3 Large is a mid-tier model in the Morph lineup, priced at $0.90 per 1M input tokens and $1.90 per 1M output tokens. It is the kind of model most teams end up defaulting to once they have measured their workloads: cheap enough to run at moderate volume, capable enough that the answer quality does not require a flagship. Morph generally publishes a budget option below this model and a flagship above it, and Morph V3 Large is the middle step in that ladder.

At $0.90 per 1M input tokens and $1.90 per 1M output tokens, the cost of a single call is dominated by output unless the workload is very output-heavy or very input-heavy in unusual ways. Output is roughly 2.1x the input cost, which is the typical shape for chat-tuned models where generation is the expensive direction. The context window is about 262K tokens, suitable for long documents and extended agent loops without having to chunk the input.
